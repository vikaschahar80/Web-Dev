Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • Skimmed milk
  • Maida
  • Butter

Recipe:

  1. Mix 1 cup milk powder, 1/4 cup flour, 1/4 tsp baking soda, 2 tbsp ghee, and enough milk to form a soft dough.
  2. Boil 2 cups sugar with 2 cups water, 1/2 tsp cardamom powder, saffron strands, and 1 tsp rose water; keep warm.
  3. Form small balls from the dough and fry until golden brown.
  4. Soak fried balls in warm sugar syrup for at least 2 hours before serving.
